## Typography scaling risk

The U27A figures were created on an approximately 18-inch-wide canvas. Reduction to a 180-mm two-column journal width gives a linear scale of approximately **0.394**. Thus, source fonts of 5.5, 7 and 10 pt would appear at approximately **2.2**, **2.8** and **3.9 pt**, respectively. This is below normal publication readability and should be verified in the generated journal-width previews.
