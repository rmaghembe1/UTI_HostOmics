## Interpretation boundary

- Transcriptomic module scores estimate pathway-program activity and should not be described as direct metabolite concentrations or actual biochemical flux.
- Tissue samples are independent GEO samples for tissue-stratified contrasts; they must not be aggregated across tissues as independent maternal replicates.
- The small UTI89-RFP and infected nonpregnant groups are best interpreted through effect sizes and confidence intervals rather than isolated p values.
